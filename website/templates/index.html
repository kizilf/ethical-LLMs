Proposed Solution

We have devised a proposed solution that incorporates a robust and secure layer to ensure reliability. Our approach includes the implementation of a GL (Guideline) checker alongside comprehensive unit tests. The GL checker serves the purpose of verifying both the functionality and ethical aspects of our solution. Furthermore, the unit tests help us determine whether any modifications have been made to the GL checker. Additionally, we employ a repetition strategy to thoroughly evaluate responses from various ethical perspectives, ensuring careful consideration for acceptance or rejection.
